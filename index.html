ABOUT
Dalubhasaan ng Lungsod ng Lucena (DLL) is a local government-funded college located in Lucena City, Philippines. It was established to provide affordable and quality higher education to residents of Lucena and nearby areas.
 DLL offers a variety of undergraduate programs aimed at equipping students with the skills and knowledge needed for professional and personal growth. The institution often focuses on fields that align with local and national development goals, such as education, business, technology, and other community-relevant courses. Its mission typically includes fostering accessible education, promoting excellence, and contributing to the socio-economic development of the region.
